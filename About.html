Charlette's Website

So what is this all about?

So, this is a website I have made for my Interactive Media class.

On this website I am going to be showing creative work in character development by blogging as my created character, Charlette Salloue. She is an inspired 24-Year-old writer, who wants to get her stories out into the world. As well as show her experience as life of a South African business owner, as she is trying to bring back to life an old book shop she has inherited.

I will also be discussing more of the courses theory work, by blogging about this website, readings, and interesting things we learn in this class.

Hope you enjoy my first website!
