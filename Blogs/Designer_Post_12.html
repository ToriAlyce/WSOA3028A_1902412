Character Rationale

In the beginning of this year when we where tasked to design our characters, I found it very difficult.

There were so many amazing characters I could do, and I got over excited and bit off more than I could chew with my first characters.

Finally, now I have made my final character. Charlette Salloue, a 24-year-old writer. The main reason I chose to make my character a writer is that I did want to take writing this year but decided to take animation instead. So, I am going to use my character to inspire me to get the best of both worlds and be able to write story’s. However, this will not be any type of stories. I really want to try bringing out how my character is truly South African, and is inspired by the country she lives in. I enjoy the added challenge of this, as it allows me to look at the beautiful aspects that South Africa has to offer and write about it as my character.

My character is very determined, and I want to also write about her challenges and experiences in being a South African business owner. Yet she is a very pessimistic person, who try’s her best to be as positive as she can. so, I thought I could enjoy from writing in her different perspectives.

I do believe I could add a lot more onto my character, and really try make her interesting. Hopefully, I am able to do that.
