Hello!

Hi, my name is Charlette Salloue. I am 24 years old, living in South Africa and I feel like a living contradiction. My natural personality is very upbeat and positive, but for some reason I also have a very cynical side. This side of me is negative, so it has always been somewhat confusing to me as I normally am quite positive. In order to deal with this, I have named my cynical side Alijah who I like to think of as a 38-year-old very grumpy English man. I am an aspiring writer and often I channel into Alijah when writing and often end up creating two stories with very different perspectives. Or even a very inspired creative work. Since this is how I write, I sign my writer’s names as “Charlette Alijah”. I hope you enjoy my stories as much as I enjoy sharing them.

I have been living in South Africa, (my whole life). I love my country and its deep varieties of culture. I recently have inherited a small secondhand book shop; I feel that it fits me as a writer to be among the things I love. I must get the book shop up and running on my own and hope to make it a safe space for people who want to come and read and learn. I am determined to make it amazing!!

Anywho… I tend to take on too much at a time so hopefully I can handle this and take you, my readers, along for the ride!
